Less Fuss. More Living

Octra was born from a simple belief: supplements should fit seamlessly into modern life. No complicated routines. No overwhelming product shelves. Just clean, effective formulas designed to deliver results—without the fuss.

Our Mission

We’re here to cut through the noise of the supplement industry. Every OCTRA product is built with purpose: supporting focus, strength, hydration, and overall wellness. We use ingredients backed by science, delivered in formats people actually enjoy taking—like gummies, powders, and more. No gimmicks. No unnecessary extras. Just the essentials, done right.

Our Values

Simplicity: Supplements should enhance your lifestyle, not complicate it.
Transparency: What you see is what you get—no hidden fillers, no misleading claims.
Inclusivity: OCTRA is designed for everyone—athletes, professionals, students, and anyone seeking balance.

Octra was founded by me, Damien, after a personal transformation that changed my life. I used to be unhealthy and overweight, struggling with consistency and balance. Through discipline, fitness, eating well, and taking the right supplements, I rebuilt my body and my lifestyle. Along the way, I realised how complicated the supplement world had become—overloaded with powders, pills, and promises. OCTRA was born to strip it back: simple, effective products that support focus, strength, and wellness every single day.

Not all supplements are created equal

At OCTRA, we believe supplements should be simple, effective, and built for modern living. That’s why every product we make is crafted with clean, high-quality ingredients in generous dosages—delivered in formats you’ll actually enjoy taking. No unnecessary fillers, no overcomplicated routines. Just straightforward formulas that support focus, strength, hydration, and overall wellness, every day.
